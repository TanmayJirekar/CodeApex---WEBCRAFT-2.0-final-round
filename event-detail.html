Event Overview

The AI/ML Hackathon brings together top minds to solve real-world challenges using machine learning, AI, and data-driven insights. Compete in teams, innovate under pressure, and win recognition from industry experts.

Date March 16–17, 2025
Category Technical
Prize Pool ₹7 Lakhs
Duration 36 Hours
Venue Tech Campus Arena

Event Rules & Guidelines

  • All teams must register before March 15, 2025.
  • Each team can have 2–4 members.
  • Use of pre-trained open-source models is allowed with credit.
  • Final submissions must include documentation and code.
  • Shortlisted teams will present to the judging panel.
  • Organizers' decision will be final and binding.
